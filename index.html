Office Procedures

Processing Finals
  1. Sort FINALS from LESSIONS
  2. Open AnyDocs, Axiom
  3. Check that FINALS are in order, remove any paperclips or staples
  4. Verify that the number of FINALS on INTAKE SHEET
  5. Place up to 15 FINALS on SCANTRON
  6. In AnyDocs, click scan
  7. Change INTAKE DATE when prompted, click ok
  8. Click Classify when done
  9. Click Extract and verify the number of scantrons counted
  10. Click Pass 1 Verify
  11. Check and fix and errors
  12. Click Commit
  13. Go to Axiom and wait 1 minute, click refresh
  14. Check that Records Awaiting Verification match number of records scanned
  15. Click Proceed to Verification
  16. If hand-graded, verify written score. If not, scan for errors in entries
  17. Repeat for all records
  18. Close when done
  19. Click refresh until all records are uploaded
  20. Stack all SCANTRONS and WRITEN WORK in order, date and rubber band
  21. Place in box
