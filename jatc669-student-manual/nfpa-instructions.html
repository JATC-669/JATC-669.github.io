NFPA Access through the Local 669 website.

  1. Log into the local 669 website: sprinklerfitters669.org
  2. Click on the “Members Only” tab (top left of page)
  3. Enter your home zip code and UA ID number: click login
  4. On the right side of this page is an area called “In This Section” click on the link called “NFPA Access”.
  5. After reading the copyright rules governing NFPA documents, there is a “Click Here” tab at the bottom of the page which will take you directly to the NFPA website.
  6. You are now logged into the National Fire Codes Subscription site.
  7. There is a tool bar across the top of the page with Blue Tabs click on the Blue Tab “PDF Access”.
  8. On this page you will be able to find all NFPA standards. You can choose what edition you want to access. Then you can choose to view the PDF online or download it your computer. It is recommended that you download it. Once you follow the prompts for downloading the Standard you will have full access to that Standard without having to login to NFPA.
