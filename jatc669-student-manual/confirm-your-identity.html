Confirm Your Identity

At the start of your apprenticeship, you will receive a personalized welcome letter from Washtenaw containing your name, address, your unique WCC student ID number and netID login.

Take a moment now to ensure you have this document.

Review your full name and address on this letter, are they spelled correctly? If not, report mistakes to Washtenaw immediately at Support_Jatc669@wccnet.edu or (866)341-8674.

Your WCC student ID number and netID login will not change and will be utilized throughout your entire apprenticeship. Note the @ symbol is part of your number and your WCC student ID number will resemble the example @00123456. This number needs to be provided in all of your correspondence with Washtenaw, do not use your social security number.
