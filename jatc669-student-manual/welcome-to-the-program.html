Welcome to the Program

You have been enrolled in a unique distance learning program developed in partnership between Sprinkler Fitters LU669 Joint-Apprenticeship Training Committee (JATC) and Washtenaw Community College (WCC).

Success requires good planning on your part in order to complete challenging courses both online and by mail during your busy years as a Sprinkler Fitter Apprentice. These courses are required in addition to many hours of on-the-job and classroom training and you are expected to complete them in adherence to JATC and WCC rules.

This guide is designed to help you understand those rules and get you started on the right track. Be sure to read now and refer to this guide throughout your apprenticeship to avoid penalties and ensure your experience is a rewarding one.
